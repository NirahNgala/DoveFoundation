HISTORY OF DOVE FOUNDATION

Dove Foundation was started in the year 2022 JUNE the 9th exactly during the eve of the 2022 elections. The first source of inspiration was the youth and service to humanity. The organisation was baptized the name DOVE to signify peace and hope. Dove Foundation is an aftermath of a realization that the values of the youth has faded away and as a result they have sought solaces to behaviours unbecoming engaging in drug abuse and many more.
